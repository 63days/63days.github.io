Bio

I am Juil Koo, a second-year PhD student in the KAIST Visual AI Group, led by Prof. Minhyuk Sung. This summer 2024, I will be joining Adobe Research as an intern, mentored by Duygu Ceylan.

My research interest lies in vision and language multi-modality, especially the relationship between 3D geometry and language. I am working towards the next advancement in AI, where machines are capable of more complex thought processes by leveraging multiple modalities, similar to humans.

My Erdős number is 3 (Juil Koo → Leonidas Guibas → Frances Yao → Paul Erdős).
